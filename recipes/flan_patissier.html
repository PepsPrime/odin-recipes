Vanilla flan pâtissier recipe

Description

A flan patissier is a french dessert, which consists mainly of a shortcrust pastry and a vanilla crème patissière

    Ingredients
  • Shortcrust pastry :
    • Flour
    • Butter
    • Eggs
    • Sugar
    • Optional : Water
  • Crème patissière :
    • Egg yolk
    • Sugar
    • Milk
    • Vanilla
    • Flour (preferably corn flour)
  • Glazing syrup :
    • Water
    • Sugar
    Steps :
  1. Shortcrust pastry :
    1. Heat your butter to room temperature and whisk it with your sugar.
    2. Add your eggs and whisk.
    3. Add your flour and with your hands, incorporate it well.
    4. Add flour or water to obtain the desired texture.
    5. Let it rest at least 1 hour protected in the fridge.
  2. Crème patissière :
    1. Melt your sugar in your eggs by whisking it
    2. Add your cornflour to your eggs and whisk. Meanwhile bring your milk (with your vanilla) to a boil.
    3. Add 1/3ish of your milk to your eggs, whisk it well then add all your milk, whisk again then bring it all back to your pot.
    4. Bring it to a boil while strongly whisking. At the first bubbles, take off your pot from the fire, and continue to whisk.
    5. Check the texture : If the cream stop moving 2 seconds after your last whisk, your crème patissière is ready.
