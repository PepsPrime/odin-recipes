Lasagna recipe

Description

A lasagna is an italian dish made of :

    Ingredients
  • Tomatoes
  • Pasta sheets
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Ground beef
  • A basic soffrito
  • Red wine
  • Tomato paste
  • Vegetable or beef stock
  • Basil, Garlic, thyme...
    Steps :
  1. Step 1 : Add Olive oil to a large pot, heat it and add your ground beef. Stir occasionnaly until browned.
  2. Step 2 : Add your diced tomatoes, your soffrito, and your aromates. Lower the heat (medium-high). After a while, add your tomato paste and dry it.
  3. Step 3 :Add your red wine and your stock. Boil for 5 minutes then lower the heat at low setting. Cover and let it simmer for a long time.
  4. Step 4 : Add an amount of butter to a saucepan, then add the same amount of flour. When well incorporated, add a small amount of milk then whisk it firmly to eliminate lumps. Bring to a boil while continuously whisking for about ~40 seconds. Season at taste.
  5. Step 5 : Oil a baking dish and add a small amount of your bolognese, then your bechamel then a pasta sheet. Repeat until no more ingredients, and keep some bechamel to go on top.
  6. Step 6 : In the oven preheated to 180Â°C, until well grilled.
